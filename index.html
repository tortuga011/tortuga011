About

I am a person with many interests, so I did a lot of different things. I was high school teacher, Coordinator for youth related projects in The municipality of Čačak and now, I'm a Front end web developer and IT manager. I've always tried to fully understand the field of work that I do and that my work meets high standards.

Web development is a fast changing field and as the field changes, so must we, the developers. With this fast paced field in mind, I try not only coding the project but understanding and improving it.

I am hardworking and a fast learner with good communication skills and reliable Web developer and IT project manager. Over the last 2 years, web programing/coding was my passion.

Languages and Frameworks: HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Vue.js, AJAX, jQuery, Bootstrap3, a little bit of PHP (php used in templates, loops, and basic functions). Tools & expertise: Git, Responsive Web Design, Agile Methodologies.
